# 💫 About Me:
Hi, I’m Manasa. I’m currently working as a Full Stack Developer, where I build cloud-native applications with APIs, dashboards, data workflows, and AI-driven features. Over the past few years, I’ve also worked across healthcare and e-commerce, which has helped me become comfortable building in different domains and solving problems from both a technical and product perspective.<br><br>I really enjoy building things that are practical, scalable, and genuinely useful. My work has taken me across backend development, frontend applications, APIs, databases, cloud systems, and AI-powered workflows. I like understanding how all the pieces come together and turning ideas into products that actually work well in the real world.<br><br>One of the most meaningful parts of my journey has been building Smart Guardian, a patented real-time AI vehicle safety system. That project came from a simple desire to use technology in a way that could help people more directly, and it later received funding and recognition. I also built Embr, an AI-assisted productivity platform focused on making everyday workflows more connected and action-oriented. Both projects reflect the kind of work I enjoy most, building technology that solves real problems in a thoughtful way.<br><br>Outside of engineering, I care a lot about communication, leadership, and continuous learning. I’ve been involved in Toastmasters, I enjoy writing, and I’m always excited to keep learning, building, and collaborating on things that create real impact.
